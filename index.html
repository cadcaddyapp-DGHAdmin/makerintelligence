Questions

Answers to what you're probably wondering

Do I need to log in to anything?
No. Reports are delivered directly to your email inbox every Monday and Friday. No dashboard. No account to check. No app to download. Just open your email.
What niche does this cover right now?
We're live with Etsy ceramics, pottery, and candle sellers. Handmade jewelry is next, followed by fiber arts. If you make ceramics or candles, you're in the right place right now — your reports are built specifically for your niche's seasonal windows, search trends, and competitor gaps.
How does the AI learn my specific shop?
After you sign up, you fill out a short onboarding form about your shop. Every Friday, you submit a 90-second update — what sold, what flopped, what you're planning. Your AI reads your updates before building Monday's report. By Week 6, it's operating with over 80% accuracy for your shop specifically. The longer you stay, the smarter it gets.
What's the difference between Starter and Growth?
Starter gives you the #1 opportunity fully unlocked every week — complete with specific actions, copy-paste tags, and seasonal timing. Growth unlocks all 3 opportunities, adds a product analysis dashboard tracking your specific listings, full buyer voice quotes with ready-to-use listing copy, the Friday performance review showing how Monday's predictions actually did, and the full seasonal capture strategy for upcoming windows.
What happens at the end of my free trial?
On Day 12 of your trial you'll receive a personal email with a link to choose your plan and keep your reports coming. If you don't choose a plan, your reports pause after Day 14. No automatic charges — ever. No credit card is collected at signup.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel anytime, no contracts. Keep in mind — when you cancel, your shop's AI history doesn't transfer. Six weeks of personalized data built around your shop stays with your subscription. The longer you stay, the more accurate and valuable your reports become.
I sell more than ceramics on my Etsy shop. Does this work?
The intelligence is built around your primary niche. If ceramics is your main category, the reports will be highly relevant even if you also sell other items. Multi-niche reporting is coming in a future tier — for now we go deep on one niche and do it right.
