Every report is generated specifically for your shop — your niche, your products, your price point, your buyer profile. After 6 weeks, your reports incorporate your historical data too, making them significantly more accurate than generic trend reports.
Do I need to log in anywhere?
No. Your reports arrive as PDF attachments directly in your inbox every Monday and Friday. Nothing to install. No dashboard to check. Just open your email.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel before your 14-day trial ends and you won't be charged a thing. Cancel any time after that and your subscription ends at the current billing period. No questions, no hoops.
What if I sell more than ceramics?
MakerIntelligence is currently optimized for Etsy ceramics and pottery sellers. Additional niches — candles, woodworking, jewelry, textiles — are in development. Enter your niche in the onboarding form and you'll be first in line when your category launches.
When exactly do reports arrive?
Monday reports arrive by 8:00 AM in your timezone — before your workday starts. Friday reports arrive by noon, giving you the weekend window to act on the opportunities. Timing is built around when makers actually work.
What's different from eRank or EverBee?
Those tools give you data and ask you to figure out what to do with it. MakerIntelligence gives you decisions. Make this. Skip that. List by Wednesday. It's a different product category entirely — intelligence delivery, not a dashboard.
